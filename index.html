History

MACHATHON 1.0

The first 3-days Machine Learning Summit and Hackathon for students in Egypt with prizes of 20,000 EGP, held in Nile University and sponsored by Vodafone, Jumia, Elmenus, Incorta and many more!

MACHATHON 1.1

An online Machine Learning Hackathon held during the start of the pandemic in aim to decrease it's spreading and increase awareness about the virus, with total prizes of 15,000 EGP sponsored by Elsevier and the help of mentors from all over the world.

MACHATHON 2.0

The first ever NLP in Arabic Language Competition, held online with total prizes of 50,000 EGP, sponsored by Incorta besides an online summit held on Eventtus with the participation of many great Engineering Companies.
